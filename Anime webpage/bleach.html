A Life Unexpected

Ichigo Kurosaki was never an ordinary high school student. His ability to interact with spirits set him apart, but he led a relatively peaceful life until a Hollow attacked his family.

In the heat of battle, a Soul Reaper named Rukia Kuchiki is severely wounded. To save his family, Ichigo accepts her offer to absorb her powers, becoming a Substitute Soul Reaper. Now, he must protect the innocent and guide souls to the afterlife.
