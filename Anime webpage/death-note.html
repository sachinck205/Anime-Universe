The Power of a Name

Light Yagami, a brilliant high school student, discovers a supernatural notebook with a terrifying power: any person whose name is written inside it dies.

The Kira Arc

Intoxicated with his new ability, Light begins a secret crusade to rid the world of criminals under the name 'Kira'. His actions attract the world's greatest detective, the mysterious L, sparking a tense battle of wits.

Light joins the investigation team to get closer to L, leading a dangerous double life. The conflict deepens with the arrival of a second Kira who idolizes the first. After a long struggle, Light manages to defeat his rival.

Justice: Absolute

Key Figures

The Players in the Game

Light Yagami

Kira

The main character who finds the Death Note. His name "Kira" comes from the English word "Killer".

L Lawliet

Detective

The main antagonist to Kira. The core of the story is the psychological "cat-and-mouse" game.

Second Kira

A famous model devoted to Kira. She possesses the "Shinigami Eyes," making her a powerful pawn.

Ryuk

Shinigami

A neutral observer who is simply enjoying the chaos Light creates. Loves apples.

Legendary Arcs

Yotsuba Arc

Corporate Kira

After Light loses his memories, a Yotsuba Group executive uses a Death Note for profit. Light and L work together to catch him.

Post-L Arc

Successors

Following a pivotal turn of events, the story introduces L's successors: the cool and calculating Near, and the impulsive Mello.

Near & Mello

The Hunt

Mello kidnaps Light’s sister to get the Death Note. Near starts working with the SPK (Special Provision for Kira) to corner Light.
