The Path to Vengeance

Devastated by grim reality, Tanjiro resolves to become a "demon slayer" so that he can turn his sister back into a human, and kill the demon who massacred his family. His journey begins when he meets Giyu Tomioka.

Despite the Corps' rule to slay all demons, Giyu is moved by Tanjiro's desperation and Nezuko's will to protect her brother. After enduring rigorous training, Tanjiro masters the "Water Breathing" sword style and officially joins the Demon Slayer Corps.
