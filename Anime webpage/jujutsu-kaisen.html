A Binding Vow

Yuji Itadori is a high school student with extraordinary physical abilities. His life takes a sudden turn when he encounters Megumi Fushiguro and unseals a high-grade cursed object to save his friends.

To defeat the curses, Yuji makes a fateful decision: he swallows the rotting finger and becomes the host for Ryomen Sukuna. Captured by Satoru Gojo, he is given a choice: be executed now, or consume all of Sukuna's fingers and die taking the Curse King with him.
