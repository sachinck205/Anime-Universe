The Great Pirate Era

The story begins with the execution of Gol D. Roger, the "Pirate King." His final words revealed the existence of the greatest treasure in the world, the One Piece, sparking a worldwide age of piracy.

Twenty-two years later, a young man named Monkey D. Luffy sets out from his home in the East Blue. Inspired by his childhood hero "Red-Haired" Shanks, Luffy dreams of finding the legendary treasure and proclaiming himself the King of the Pirates.
